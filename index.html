THE CALCULATOR APP

Rules

1. Use input textbox to operate.
2. Give a input string and press "Enter" or "=" or click "=" to get the solution within the textbox itself.
3. You can use both keyboard and mouse (click the numbers and symbols).
4. Give Expression in the form of "operand operator operand" to get a valid result. For Example,

"1 * 2" and press "enter" or "=" (or) click "=". //with space in between the operand and operator


5.Press "Esc" to clear the input textbox.
